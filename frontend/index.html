ABOUT SATYA

What is SATYA?

SATYA (Sanskrit: Truth) is an AI-powered misinformation detection platform that analyzes text, news headlines, and WhatsApp forwards in real-time. It cross-checks content across 40+ verified news sources using semantic AI analysis to determine credibility.

Philosophy

सत्यमेव जयते — Truth Alone Triumphs. This ancient principle from the Mundaka Upanishad is the founding philosophy of this project. In the Mahabharata, Krishna served as the ultimate guide to truth. SATYA is designed to be the digital equivalent — cutting through misinformation to reveal reality.

How it works

SATYA uses a 5-signal verification pipeline to calculate a credibility score from 0–100:

01 AI Text Detection
02 Google Fact Check
03 RSS News Feeds
04 DuckDuckGo Search
05 Wikipedia Validation
06 Semantic Analysis
